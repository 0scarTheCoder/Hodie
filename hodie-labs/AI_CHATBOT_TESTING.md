### Testing Methodology

#### **Criteria for Evaluation:**
1. **Accuracy** (1-10): How medically sound are the recommendations?
2. **Personalization** (1-10): How tailored is the response to the user?
3. **Conversational Flow** (1-10): How natural and engaging is the interaction?
4. **Actionability** (1-10): How specific and implementable are the suggestions?
5. **Comprehensiveness** (1-10): How complete is the response?
6. **User Experience** (1-10): How pleasant is the interaction?

#### **Test Protocol:**
1. Ask identical questions to both chatbots
2. Record responses verbatim
3. Rate each response on the 6 criteria
4. Note response time and character limits
5. Test edge cases and error handling
6. Evaluate follow-up question capabilities
